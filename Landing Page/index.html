My Place Pune
Educational city

Pune, one of India's top cities, provides something for everyone, regardless of age.
This metropolis is flecked with shops, theme parks,
clubs, and much more, keeping up with modernity.

The landscape of Pune district is distributed triangularly in western Maharashtra at the foothills of the Sahyadri Mountains
and is divided into three parts:
“Ghatmatha”, “Maval” and “Desh”. Pune district forms a part of the tropical monsoon land and
therefore shows a significant seasonal variation in temperature as well as rainfall conditions.
